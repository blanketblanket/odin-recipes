Spam Fried Rice

Description

An easy meal for depression days.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Peanut oil
  • 1/2 can Spam
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups Frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 cups Day old rice
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cube spam
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in pan and fry the spam
  3. Dice onions and crush garlic.
  4. Heat 2 tbsp oil in pan and fry onions and garlic
  5. Add frozen veg and fry until defrosted
  6. Add rice and spam. Stir until warm through
  7. Make a hole in the middle and crack the egg in, let it fry a bit before stirring through.
  8. Add soy sauce and stirr through
