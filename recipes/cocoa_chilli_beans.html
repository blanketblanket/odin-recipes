Cocoa Chilli Beans

Description

Adding cocoa powder to chilli beans gives them a rich earthy flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 can Diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 can Kidney beans
  • 2 tbsp Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Dice onions and crush garlic.
  2. Heat oil in pan and fry onions and garlic
  3. Add diced tomatoes and chilli powder, cumin, sald and pepper
  4. Heat until bubbling, add kidney beans and cocoa powder.
  5. Cook on medium until beans are soft and ready to eat.
