Mystery Soup

Description

This is not really a recipe. It's more of a set of guidelines for a bunch of different soups.

Ingredients

  • Dry source of umami (kelp, dried dashi powder, mushroom)
  • Wet source of umami (tomatoes, shrimp paste, bean paste)
  • Salt source (salt, soy sauce, fish sauce)
  • Veg (cabbage, carrots, green veg)
  • Carbohydrate (rice, noodles, pasta)
  • Protein (tofu, chicken, oysters, mince, fish balls)
  • Aromatic (chives, parsley, coriander, garlic)

Steps

  1. Boil dry source of umami for a while until flavour is infused. Chuck the dry source of umami
  2. Add wet source of umami to taste
  3. Add veg and carb and simmer until nearly ready to eat
  4. Add protein
  5. Add chopped aromatic before serving
